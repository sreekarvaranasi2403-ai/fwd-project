??Health Tips For Seniors

Simple daily advice for a healthier routine.

Blood Pressure Care

  • Check blood pressure at the same time daily.
  • Reduce extra salt in food.
  • Take short walks if your doctor allows.

Diabetes Support

  • Do not skip meals.
  • Check sugar level as advised.
  • Keep healthy snacks ready.

General Wellness

  • Drink enough water through the day.
  • Sleep well and avoid late-night stress.
  • Talk to your pharmacist if you feel unsure.

Small daily habits make a big difference in long-term health.
